void sys_clock_init_common ( void  )

Called once from sys_clock_init. Initialization of source_tasklet here.

  1. Initialize all global variables.
  2. Initialize two lists : notify_list and source_list and its semaphores.
  3. Initialize tasklet source_tasklet with callback source_tasklet_callback and without data. This tasklet will be scheduled in function sysclk_source_tick.
